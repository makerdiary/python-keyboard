Hand-wiring a Keyboard
======================

## Materials
+ 0.8mm brass wire
+ 61+ switches
+ keybaord plate
+ plate mount stabilizers
+ 61+ diodes for anti-ghost
+ [Pitaya Go](https://github.com/makerdiary/pitaya-go)

## Tools
+ soldering iron
+ solder
+ tweezer
+ multimeter
